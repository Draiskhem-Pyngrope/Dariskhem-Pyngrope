About Me

Hello! I am a doctoral researcher specializing in compact modeling (BSIM and ASM-HEMT), simulation, and material‚Äìdevice co-design of wide and ultra-wide bandgap semiconductors for next-generation power and RF electronics. My work focuses on physics-based and SPICE-compatible models for advanced devices such as GaN HEMTs, ferroelectric HEMTs, and multi-gate FETs, with contributions to industry-standard modeling frameworks BSIM-CMG-NN-Assist-BDMC1.0.0 . I also explore AI-assisted device modeling and Ga2O3 thin-film fabrication using RF sputtering, bridging material engineering with circuit-level reliability.
